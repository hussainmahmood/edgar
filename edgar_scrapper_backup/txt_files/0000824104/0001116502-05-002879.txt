ITEM 3.  SOURCE AND AMOUNT OF FUNDS.

         The shares of Common Stock of the Issuer were acquired by
Nextelligence, Inc. in exchange for all of its shares of common stock of Global
Portals Online, Inc. No funds were borrowed by Nextelligence, Inc., to acquire
shares of Common Stock of the Issuer.


________________________________________________________________________________
ITEM 4.  PURPOSE OF THE TRANSACTION.

         Nextelligence, Inc. acquired its shares of Common Stock of the Issuer
in connection with the exchange of all of its shares of common stock of Global
Portals Online, Inc. for shares of Common Stock of the Issuer. Nextelligence,
Inc. does not at present have any plans or proposals which relate to or would
result in any of the actions specified in clauses (a) through (j) of Item 4 of
Schedule 13D. Nextelligence, Inc. reserves the right from time to time to
acquire additional shares of Common Stock of the Issuer, to dispose of shares of
Common Stock of the Issuer, and to engage in any or all of the actions specified
in clauses (a) through (j) of Item 4 of Schedule 13D.
